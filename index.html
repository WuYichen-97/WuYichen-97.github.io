Biography

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Prof. Ying Wei and Prof. Prof. Kede Ma. Before joining CityU, I received my M.Sc degree under the supervision of Prof. Deyu Meng from the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University, in 2021, and dual B.S. degrees from Beijing Jiaotong University's Schools of Mathematics and Statistics, and Economics and Management in 2018.

Research Interest

My research interests include continual learning, transfer learning, and meta-learning. Currently, I am focusing on designing an algorithm that can adapt to new tasks and environments while avoiding issues such as forgetting and hallucination. Additionally, I am dedicated to investigating the following research topics:
  • Forgetting/hallucination problems of large pre-trained model
  • Knowledge Transfer in the streaming tasks
  • Weakly Supervised Learning (including noisy/imbalanced settings)
